Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to order Domino’s Pizza?

    • Ordering on our platform is very easy. You can order directly or create a profile, then select between delivery or carry out, choose your store, pick your favorite meal, pay and its done! If you need any help, feel free to call your store directly. See list of stores here.

  • How much is your delivery fee?

    • Our delivery is absolutely free, however we only accept delivery for orders of 3’000 ₦ and above.

  • Do you deliver to my address?

    • In order to find out if we deliver to you, type your address into delivery or contact the restaurant close to you.

    Getting a hot pizza to your door is our uncompromising quality commitment, that is why we have carefully selected delivery zones. We are also working to bring Domino's Pizza close to you.

  • What is your pizza menu? What else do you sell on top of pizza?

    • We offer a large variety of hot & fresh pizza with different toppings, as well as delicious chicken, bread & dessert sides

    Download our Lagos & Ibadan menu here.

    Download our Abuja menu here.

  • Do you have any promos?

    • We always have great promos to offer you, check our promo section and follow us on social media to be first notified for any good deals.

  • Do you accept payment via bank transfer?

    • At Domino’s Pizza Nigeria, we never accept payment from customers via bank transfer. Our payment options are: online payment, cash or POS.

  • How safe is it to use my card to pay on your platform?

    • It is absolutely safe. We are working with the best and most secure payment platform partners. If you have any issues with paying with your card, please send an email to info@dominos.ng.

  • I am not satisfied with my pizza purchase, what can I do?

    • At Domino’s Pizza, we are always Hungry to Be Better and love to hear from you. Write us an email: info@dominos.ng with your feedback and we will make it right for you.

  • What is the Pizza Tracker and how does it work?

    • The Pizza Tracker lets you know where your pizza is right now! Enter your phone number to see the status of your order.

  • Why should I create a Pizza Profile?

    • Are you hungry and don't want to waste time? With your Pizza Profile on dominos.ng you can order pizza within seconds, access your last 5 orders and don’t need to type your address ever again.

  • Can I save my delivery addresses and my recent orders?

    • Yes, you can! Take a moment and create a Pizza Profile on dominos.ng. On your personal profile you have the ability to manage delivery addresses and recent orders and re-order within a few seconds!

  • Is my login data from the old website still valid?

    • Yes, your old login data is still valid.

  • Does Domino's Pizza protect my personal data?

    • Yes, absolutely! At Domino’s Pizza, we treat your information confidentially and in accordance with all data protection guidelines. Learn more about this: privacy.
